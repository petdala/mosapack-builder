Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MosaPack

What happens after I preview my mosaic?

Start with a proof request. You can choose:

  • Custom Proof Paths: Sticker-ready proofs come first. Magnetic and premium display formats can be reviewed after proof approval.
  • Digital Review Path: Use the preview builder to decide whether the photo is a good fit before requesting review.

Both paths begin with the same preview. Compare options here or try our free builder.

Are physical formats available from the preview alone?

No. Physical formats are reviewed after proof approval. Pricing and timing are confirmed only after feasibility review.

What if I'm not satisfied with my mosaic?

If a photo is not suitable for a clear mosaic proof, we will follow up before recommending any next step.

Is payment collected in the preview builder?

No. The builder creates a free preview and proof request. No payment is collected in the preview builder.

What photo works best?

Photos with good contrast and clear subjects work best. Portraits, pets, and landscapes are ideal. Our free preview tool shows how your photo can translate before you request a proof.

Can I review a magnetic format?

Magnetic formats can be discussed after proof approval. They are not started from the preview alone.

When are pricing and timing confirmed?

Pricing and timing are confirmed after proof review and feasibility review, not during the free preview step.

How does color matching work?

MosaPack uses a professional color science workflow to map your photo into available mosaic colors more accurately than simple RGB guessing.

What physical formats can I review?

Start with a sticker-ready mosaic proof. Magnetic and premium display options can be discussed after the proof is reviewed and feasibility is confirmed.
