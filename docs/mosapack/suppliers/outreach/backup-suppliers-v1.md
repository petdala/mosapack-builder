# Backup Supplier RFQ Notes v1

Date: 2026-07-09
Status: backup outreach notes.

Do not use archived price tables as facts. Any price, tolerance, or capability must come from a current quote, current vendor page, or physical sample.

## Sticker Mule

Why contact:

- known sticker vendor
- possible fast sample turnaround
- useful quote comparison

Why not primary:

- dense 0.5 inch rounded-square grid support is unknown
- may be optimized for larger stickers, not hundreds of tiny cells
- kitting and board support unknown

Exact unknowns:

- can they kiss-cut hundreds of 0.5 inch rounded squares on one sheet?
- can they accept press-ready PDF/dieline layers?
- registration tolerance across dense sheets
- sample cost and lead time

Sample questions:

- Can you produce one dense mosaic sticker sheet sample?
- What minimum gap is required between cuts?
- What is your smallest reliable rounded-square kiss-cut size?
- Do you recommend matte or another material for hand placement?

## Sticker Ninja

Why contact:

- backup quality quote
- possible strong color and cut precision
- useful if OnlineLabels/local shop cannot meet tolerance

Why not primary:

- capability claims are not current MosaPack evidence
- MOQ, sample terms, and exact geometry support need confirmation

Exact unknowns:

- 0.5 inch rounded-square dense-grid support
- registration tolerance
- color consistency on tiny cells
- sample and reorder economics

Sample questions:

- Can you quote a 0.5 inch rounded-square dense sticker sheet?
- What tolerance can you hold for print-to-cut?
- Can you produce a sample from synthetic press-ready PDF artwork?
- What matte/low-glare material do you recommend?

## Printful

Why contact:

- useful reference-only POD comparison
- could be valuable if exact dense sheet support exists

Why not primary:

- current capability for dense 0.5 inch cell-grid sheets is unproven
- old Printful specs are archived research, not current truth
- supplier API work is out of scope

Exact unknowns:

- whether required sheet layout is supported
- whether press-ready PDF/dense die-cut geometry is accepted
- whether one-off custom sheets can be produced without public integration work

Sample questions:

- Can you produce 0.5 inch rounded-square kiss-cut cells in a dense grid?
- What maximum number of distinct kiss-cut cells can one sheet contain?
- Can each customer design be unique?
- Is this compatible with manual order upload, not API integration?

## Printify / Gooten

Why contact:

- low-priority fallback POD comparison
- broad supplier network could surface a specialist

Why not primary:

- exact production partner capabilities are unclear
- platform-level catalog may not support custom dense cell sheets
- integration is out of scope

Exact unknowns:

- which underlying print provider can support MosaPack geometry
- whether small custom cells are available
- whether sample proofing is possible

Sample questions:

- Do any network providers support dense kiss-cut sticker sheets?
- Can the provider accept press-ready PDF artwork?
- Can samples be ordered manually?
- Can provider details and tolerances be disclosed before ordering?
