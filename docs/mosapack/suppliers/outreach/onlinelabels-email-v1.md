## Concise First Email

Subject: Feasibility: 0.5" kiss-cut rounded-square sticker mosaic sheets

Hello,

I'm evaluating suppliers for a buildable sticker-mosaic proof product. The product uses 0.5" rounded-square sticker cells arranged into a custom photo mosaic. The current launch path is sticker-ready proof first, with no customer payment collected until after proof approval.

I'm looking to confirm whether your team can produce sample sheets with:

- full-color printed sticker cells
- 0.5" rounded-square kiss-cuts
- dense grid layout with many unique cell colors per sheet
- press-ready PDF artwork supplied by us
- matte/low-glare material preferred
- tight print-to-cut registration

Could you confirm:

1. Can you kiss-cut hundreds of 0.5" rounded-square stickers on one sheet?
2. What registration tolerance can you hold across the sheet?
3. Can you accept press-ready PDFs rather than using an online design editor?
4. What sheet materials do you recommend for easy peel-and-place craft use?
5. Can you provide a die-cut physical proof/sample?
6. What are sample cost, MOQ, lead time, and reorder terms?
7. Can you support repeat custom designs at low quantity?
8. Can you support kitting or only printed sheets?

I can send a small synthetic sample packet with no customer data.

Thanks,
Derek
