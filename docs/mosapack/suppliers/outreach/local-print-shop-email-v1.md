# Local Print Shop Outreach Email v1

Status: sendable first-contact draft.

## Positioning

Local print shops are a rush backup, physical validation partner, and possible kitting/local assembly helper. They are not the primary scale path unless quote and sample results prove viability.

## First Email

Subject: Local sample request: 0.5" kiss-cut sticker mosaic sheets

Hello,

I'm validating a proof-first sticker mosaic product and looking for a local print/die-cut partner for short-run samples. This is not a production order yet; I need to learn whether the geometry and materials are feasible.

The sample target is:

- full-color sticker sheets
- 0.5" rounded-square kiss-cut cells
- dense grid layout with many unique colors
- press-ready PDF artwork supplied by us
- matte or low-glare sticker material
- tight print-to-cut registration

Could you tell me whether you can support a small sample run and quote:

1. label/sticker sheet printing
2. kiss-cutting 0.5" rounded squares
3. registration tolerance across a dense sheet
4. matte sticker material options
5. setup or die fees
6. local pickup timing
7. board printing or mounting
8. kitting / packaging support

The sample artwork is synthetic and contains no customer data.

Thanks,
Derek

## Follow-Up Questions

- Can you cut hundreds of 0.5" rounded-square cells on one sheet?
- Do you prefer vector cut paths, separate dieline layers, or another format?
- What bleed do you require for full-color cells?
- Can you test 0.03 inch and 0.05 inch bleed?
- Can you source or print a 12 inch placement board / black-base board?
- Can you pack sheets, board, and instruction card together?
- What are sample, 10-unit, and 100-unit quote ranges?

## Validation Role

A local shop can be viable as:

- rush backup
- sample validation partner
- board printing partner
- kitting partner
- local pickup option

Do not treat local shop feasibility as public production readiness until samples pass.
