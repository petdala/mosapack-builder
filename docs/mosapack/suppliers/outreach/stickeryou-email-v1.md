# StickerYou Outreach Email v1

Status: sendable first-contact draft.

## First Email

Subject: Feasibility: custom 0.5" sticker mosaic sheets and magnet samples

Hello,

I'm evaluating suppliers for a proof-first custom sticker mosaic product. The current launch path is sticker-ready proof first. Magnet interest is being evaluated separately and is not an active production promise.

I need to confirm whether your team can support sample production for:

A. Sticker sheets
B. Magnet sheets / magnetic tiles
C. Possible kitting or dropship support

The sample artwork is synthetic and contains no customer data.

## A. Sticker Sheet Questions

1. Can you produce 0.5" rounded-square kiss-cut sticker cells?
2. Can one sheet contain a dense grid with many unique full-color cells?
3. Can you accept press-ready PDF artwork and dielines?
4. What registration tolerance can you hold?
5. What material do you recommend for matte, easy peel-and-place craft use?
6. Can you provide a physical sample?
7. What are sample cost, MOQ, lead time, and reorder terms?

## B. Magnet Questions

1. Can you produce 0.5" rounded-square magnetic pieces or magnetic sticker cells?
2. What is your minimum reliable magnet piece size?
3. Are 0.5" magnetic cells hand-placeable by customers?
4. Can magnetic material be sheeted and kiss-cut?
5. Can you support a magnetic board or backing substrate?
6. What packaging or safety constraints apply to small magnetic pieces?

## C. Kitting / Dropship Questions

1. Can you package sheets by color, section, or sequence?
2. Can you include an instruction card or placement guide?
3. Can you include a board/backing component?
4. Is fulfillment true custom POD, inventory/pick-pack, or manual project work?
5. Can you dropship only printed sheets, or full kits too?

## Guardrail

We are not assuming StickerYou can dropship the final product. We need an actual quote and physical sample before any production claim.

Derek
