# StickerYou Magnet / Sticker RFQ v1

Date: 2026-07-09
Status: supplier feasibility inquiry; not a production order.

## Purpose

Ask StickerYou or a similar sticker/magnet vendor whether MosaPack's sticker-ready proof output can translate into sticker and future magnet samples.

## Current Public Position

MosaPack is proof-first. Sticker-ready proof is the recommended first path. Magnet interest is metadata for follow-up, not an active production promise.

## Sticker Questions

1. Can you produce `0.5in` rounded-square kiss-cut sticker cells?
2. Can you print custom mixed sheets per design?
3. Can you support edge-to-edge color with `0.03in` or `0.05in` bleed?
4. What registration tolerance can you hold?
5. What materials are available for matte, removable, permanent, and weatherproof use?
6. Can you package sheets by color, section, or sequence?

## Magnet Questions

1. Can you produce `0.5in` rounded-square magnetic pieces or magnetic sticker cells?
2. What is the minimum reliable magnet piece size?
3. Are `0.5in` magnetic cells hand-placeable by customers?
4. Can magnetic material be sheeted and kiss-cut?
5. Can you support a magnetic board or backing substrate?
6. What packaging constraints apply to small magnetic pieces?

## Stock / Hybrid Questions

1. Can you print full solid-color sheets per approved color?
2. Is solid-color sheet fulfillment true print-on-demand or inventory/pick-pack?
3. Can stock sheets be combined with a custom mixed top-off sheet?
4. Can sheets be labeled by color ID/name and packed with a numbered guide?

## Quote Request

Please quote sample feasibility only. MosaPack is not placing a production order from this RFQ.
