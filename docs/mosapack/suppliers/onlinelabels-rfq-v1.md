# OnlineLabels RFQ v1

Date: 2026-07-09
Status: supplier feasibility inquiry; not a production order.

## Purpose

Ask OnlineLabels whether MosaPack can source or test sticker-sheet geometry compatible with the current proof-first validation path.

## Context

MosaPack is validating a buildable sticker mosaic proof system. Public customer flow is proof-first; no checkout, pricing, shipping, or production promise is active from this RFQ.

## Feasibility Questions

1. Can you supply blank label stock suitable for `0.5in` rounded-square sticker cells?
2. Can you confirm material options for matte paper, weatherproof polyester, removable adhesive, and permanent adhesive?
3. Can you provide exact printable area and unprintable margin guidance for US Letter sheets?
4. Can you provide die/kiss-cut tolerance information for `0.5in` rounded-square cells?
5. Can you confirm whether `0.03in` or `0.05in` bleed is safer for edge-to-edge color?
6. Can you provide sample sheets for plain-paper overlay and label-stock validation?
7. Can you provide cost per blank sheet at sample, 10, 100, and 500 sheet quantities?
8. Can you provide storage, shelf-life, curl, and printer compatibility notes?

## Data Requested

For each material, provide:

- product URL or SKU
- sheet capacity / geometry
- material
- adhesive
- printer compatibility
- cost breaks
- sample availability
- lead time
- notes on handling or peelability

## Current MosaPack Guardrail

OnlineLabels inquiry is validation support only. It does not authorize public production, shipping promises, or checkout.
