# Fine Cell Supplier Questions

Date: 2026-07-07
Status: supplier discovery checklist; no fine-cell runtime output implemented.

## Purpose

Capture supplier questions before MosaPack considers 0.375in or 0.25in sticker/magnet cell profiles.

## Questions

1. Can you produce `0.375in` rounded-square stickers?
2. Can you produce `0.25in` rounded-square stickers?
3. What is your minimum kiss-cut size?
4. What is your minimum safe gap between kiss cuts?
5. What registration tolerance can you hold across a full sheet?
6. How does registration vary across sheet corners and between sheets?
7. How peelable are 0.375in cells by hand?
8. How peelable are 0.25in cells by hand?
9. Can customers place these manually without tweezers or transfer film?
10. What materials are available at these sizes?
11. Are matte, weatherproof, vinyl/polyester, and magnet materials supported?
12. Can sheets be packaged by color?
13. Can sheets be packaged by section?
14. Can full solid-color sheets and mixed top-off sheets be packed together?
15. What is the MOQ for 0.375in samples?
16. What is the MOQ for 0.25in samples?
17. What is the sample cost and lead time?
18. Do you offer vendor assembly, transfer film, or pre-mounted panels?
19. Can you produce a numbered board/map?
20. Do you support client-owned preprinted stock?

## Decision Rule

Do not expose fine-cell options publicly until supplier answers and physical sample evidence prove the format is buildable.
