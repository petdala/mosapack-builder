## Detailed Technical Inquiry For Positive Responses

Subject: RFQ: 0.5in rounded-square sticker mosaic kit sample

Hello,

I am validating a sticker mosaic kit format. The kit uses `0.5in` rounded-square sticker cells arranged into a buildable image.

Can you review a synthetic sample packet and answer the questions below?

### Production Feasibility

- Can you produce or test `0.5in` rounded-square sticker sheets?
- Can you print custom mixed sheets per order?
- Can you print full solid-color sheets per order?
- Is solid-color sheet production true print-on-demand or inventory/pick-pack?
- Can you support a hybrid model with stock solid-color sheets plus a custom mixed top-off sheet?
- Can you create 24x24 / 12in starter kits?
- Can you support 32x32 / 16in later?
- Can you support 0.375in or 0.25in cells later?

### Samples and Cost

- What is the sample cost?
- What is the MOQ?
- What is the expected unit cost at low volume?
- What is the lead time for samples?
- What is the lead time for repeat orders?

### Materials and Geometry

- What material options are available?
- Do you recommend matte label, weatherproof polyester, removable adhesive, or permanent adhesive?
- What registration tolerance can you hold?
- What bleed do you require around 0.5in cells?
- Can you compare `0.03in` vs `0.05in` bleed?
- Can the cells be hand-peeled without tearing or curling?

### Packaging and Assembly

- Can you package by color or section?
- Can you label sheets by color ID/name?
- Can you include numbered board/map inserts?
- Can you provide pick/pack support for full color sheets?
- Can you store client-owned preprinted stock?
- Do you charge storage or pick/pack fees?

### File Requirements

- Do you prefer PDF, SVG, or another production format?
- Can you use manifest JSON or a color legend for QA?
- Are there constraints around small grid artwork?

The current packet is synthetic/demo-only and is not a customer order.

Thank you,
Derek
