# 📋 Foam Board & Cork Tiles Assembly Guide
**How Non-Magnetic Backing Works for Mosaic Stickers**

**Last Updated:** October 19, 2025

---

## 🎯 **THE CONCEPT:**

Instead of making mosaic tiles **magnetic**, you apply the sticker sheet to a **lightweight backing board** and hang it on the wall like a poster.

**Think of it like:**
- Poster + backing = sturdier than paper
- Photo print + foam board = professional mounting
- Sticker sheet + backing = finished mosaic art

---

## 📋 **OPTION 1: FOAM BOARD (FOAM CORE BOARD)**

### **What Is Foam Board?**

**Physical Structure:**
- Two layers of **smooth paper/cardstock**
- Middle layer of **lightweight foam** (usually polystyrene)
- Total thickness: 3/16" (about 5mm)

**Visual:**
```
┌─────────────────┐ ← Top paper layer (smooth white)
│▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒│ ← Foam core (lightweight)
└─────────────────┘ ← Bottom paper layer
```

**Common Sizes:**
- 16"×20" (perfect for mosaic)
- 20"×30"
- 24"×36"

**Where It's Used:**
- School presentations
- Photography mounting
- Craft projects
- Signs and displays

---

### **How Customer Uses Foam Board:**

#### **Step 1: Receive Sticker Sheet from You**
- Custom mosaic printed as kiss-cut stickers
- Arrives in mail (Printful ships)
- 8.5×11" sticker sheet with numbered tiles

#### **Step 2: Buy Foam Board from Amazon**
- Order foam board pack (10 sheets for ~$19)
- Uses 1 sheet per mosaic
- Prime shipping (arrives in 1-2 days)

#### **Step 3: Apply Sticker Sheet to Foam Board**

**Assembly (2 minutes):**

1. **Lay foam board on flat surface**
   - White side facing up
   - Clean surface (no dust)

2. **Peel backing from sticker sheet**
   - Entire sheet has adhesive backing
   - OR stickers are individually kiss-cut (peel one by one)

3. **Apply sticker sheet to foam board**
   - Start at one edge
   - Smooth out bubbles with credit card
   - Press firmly

4. **Done!**
   - Mosaic is now mounted on rigid backing
   - Ready to display

#### **Step 4: Display**

**Option A: Hang on Wall**
- Use **Command Strips** (Amazon affiliate!)
- Stick to back of foam board
- Press to wall
- No nail holes needed

**Option B: Prop on Easel**
- Buy small easel (Amazon affiliate!)
- Place on desk/shelf
- Professional presentation

**Option C: Frame It**
- Buy 16×20" frame (Amazon affiliate!)
- Insert foam board mosaic
- Hang like regular art

---

### **Advantages of Foam Board:**

✅ **Lightweight** - Won't fall off command strips
✅ **Rigid** - Won't curl or bend like paper
✅ **Clean edges** - Professional look
✅ **Cheap** - $2 per board (from pack)
✅ **Easy to cut** - Scissors or box cutter if need custom size
✅ **Reusable** - Can change sticker designs

### **Disadvantages:**

❌ **Not magnetic** - Can't stick to fridge
❌ **Less durable than wood** - Can dent if hit
❌ **Not weatherproof** - Indoor use only

## 🪵 **OPTION 2: CORK TILES**

### **What Are Cork Tiles?**

**Physical Structure:**
- **Cork material** (same as wine corks, bulletin boards)
- Natural tan/brown color
- Textured surface
- Self-adhesive backing (some versions)

**Thickness:** 1/4" (about 6mm)

**Common Sizes:**
- 12"×12" tiles
- Pack of 4 or 8 tiles

**Where It's Used:**
- Bulletin boards
- Wall decor
- Sound dampening
- Pin boards

---

### **How Customer Uses Cork Tiles:**

#### **Assembly (2 minutes):**

1. **Receive sticker sheet from you**
   - Custom mosaic kiss-cut stickers

2. **Buy cork tiles from Amazon**
   - Self-adhesive cork tiles (12×12")
   - Uses 1 tile per mosaic

3. **Apply sticker sheet to cork surface**
   - Peel backing from sticker sheet
   - Apply to textured cork surface
   - Press firmly (texture helps adhesion)

4. **Display:**
   - **Hang:** Command strips on back
   - **Pin:** Can add push pins around edges for decoration
   - **Layer:** Multiple cork tiles = larger mosaic

---

### **Advantages of Cork:**

✅ **Textured aesthetic** - Rustic, warm look
✅ **Can add pins** - Decorate with push pins
✅ **Sound dampening** - Absorbs noise
✅ **Natural material** - Eco-friendly vibe
✅ **Forgiving surface** - Hides imperfections

### **Disadvantages:**

❌ **Textured surface** - Stickers may not lay perfectly flat
❌ **Brown color shows through** - Not ideal for white backgrounds
❌ **Heavier than foam** - Needs stronger mounting
❌ **More expensive** - $4-5 per tile vs $2 foam board

---

## 🆚 **FOAM BOARD vs CORK TILES COMPARISON:**

| Feature | Foam Board | Cork Tiles |
|---------|------------|------------|
| **Price** | $2/board | $4-5/tile |
| **Weight** | Very light | Light-medium |
| **Surface** | Smooth white | Textured tan |
| **Aesthetic** | Clean, modern | Rustic, warm |
| **Mounting** | Command strips (easy) | Command strips (needs stronger) |
| **Customization** | Can paint edges | Can add pins |
| **Best For** | Clean look, budget | Rustic vibe, pinboard style |

## 🧲 **WHY NOT JUST MAKE EVERYTHING MAGNETIC?**

**Good question! Here's why foam/cork exist:**

### **Magnetic Backing:**
- **Pros:** Stick to fridge, reusable, repositionable
- **Cons:** More expensive ($5 vs $2), limited display locations (needs metal surface)

### **Foam/Cork Backing:**
- **Pros:** Cheaper, hang anywhere, lightweight, professional look
- **Cons:** Not repositionable, needs command strips/frame

**Customer Choice:**
- **Fridge lovers** → Magnetic backing ($29 total)
- **Wall art lovers** → Foam board ($26 total)
- **Rustic decor lovers** → Cork tiles ($28 total)
