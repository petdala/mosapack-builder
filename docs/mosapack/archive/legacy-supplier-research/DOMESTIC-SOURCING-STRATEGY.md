### Break-Even Analysis: DIY Manufacturing

**Scenario:** Buy brick molds, manufacture via US injection molding

**Startup Costs:**
- Brick molds: $20,000 (one-time)
- Injection molding setup: $5,000
- Testing/iteration: $2,000
- Legal (IP attorney): $2,000
- **Total Startup:** $29,000

**Per-Unit Costs:**
- Raw ABS plastic: $0.15/brick × 300 = $45.00/kit
- Injection molding labor: $5.00/kit
- Shipping from molder: $2.00/kit
- **Total COGS:** $52.00/kit

**Break-Even:**
- Savings vs WoBrick with tariff: $60.30 - $52.00 = $8.30/kit
- Break-even units: $29,000 ÷ $8.30 = **3,494 kits**
- At 90 kits/year: **39 years** ❌ Not viable initially

**Revised Break-Even (Scale Assumptions):**
- Year 2: 200 kits
- Year 3: 400 kits
- Year 4: 600 kits
- **Break-even: Year 8** ⚠️ Long timeline

**Conclusion:** DIY manufacturing only viable if:
1. Annual volume reaches 500+ kits (Year 3+)
2. Tariffs increase above 75% (making WoBrick unviable)
3. No US/Mexico suppliers available at reasonable cost

**Recommendation:** Pursue Mexico contract manufacturing first, keep DIY as backup plan.

## Risk Assessment: Domestic Sourcing

### Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Higher base costs | High | Medium | Price increase to $119, absorb in margin |
| Quality differences | Medium | High | Thorough testing, samples before bulk orders |
| Longer lead times (US) | Medium | Low | Order further in advance, keep buffer stock |
| MOQ too high | Medium | Medium | Split orders with other buyers, start small |
| No LEGO-compatible US option | Medium | High | Mexico sourcing, alternative product designs |
| IP legal issues (DIY) | Low | Critical | IP attorney review before any manufacturing |

### Contingency Plans

**If no viable US/USMCA brick supplier found:**
- Maintain WoBrick relationship, absorb tariff in pricing
- Focus growth on Magnet Kit (tariff-free)
- Develop alternative product (acrylic, magnetic, wood)
- Position Brick Kit as ultra-premium low-volume offering

**If tariffs increase above 75%:**
- Immediately halt Brick Kit sales
- Offer "Brick Kit Alternative" with acrylic/wood/magnetic tiles
- Full pivot to Magnet Kit as core product
- Cricut die-cut magnets become primary offering
