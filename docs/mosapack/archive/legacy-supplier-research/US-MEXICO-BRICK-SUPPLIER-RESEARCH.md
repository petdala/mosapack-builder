## COST COMPARISON ANALYSIS

### Current Situation (WoBrick + Tariff)

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| WoBrick bricks (32×32) | $44.00 |
| Tariff (34%) | $15.30 |

---

### US Manufacturing Cost Estimate

**Assumptions:**
- Custom mold costs: $10,000-30,000 (one-time)
- Per-piece costs: $0.10-0.20/brick for ABS plastic
- 1,024 bricks per kit (32×32 mosaic)
- Labor/overhead: 15-20% markup

**Estimated Cost Breakdown:**

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Raw ABS plastic (1,024 bricks @ $0.15 ea) | $45.00 |
| Labor & overhead (20%) | $9.00 |
| **Estimated Total per Kit** | **$54.00** |
| **Tariff** | **$0** (Made in USA) |
| **All-in Cost** | **$54.00** |

**Savings vs WoBrick+Tariff:** $5.30/kit (9% reduction)
**Annual Savings (90 kits):** $477/year

**Mold Amortization:**
- If mold costs $20,000, break-even at 3,774 kits
- At 90 kits/year, break-even in 42 years ❌

**Conclusion:** US manufacturing only viable if:
1. Mold costs are shared/reduced
2. Volume increases significantly (500+ kits/year)
3. Per-piece costs are lower than estimated ($0.10/brick or less)

---

### Mexico Manufacturing Cost Estimate

**Assumptions:**
- 20-40% lower labor costs than US
- Same mold costs ($10K-30K)
- USMCA = 0% tariff
- Shipping slightly higher than US but WAY lower than China

**Estimated Cost Breakdown:**

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Raw ABS plastic (1,024 bricks @ $0.12 ea) | $36.00 |

**Conclusion:** Mexico manufacturing more viable than US, but still requires:

3. Accurate per-piece pricing (need real quotes)

## RED FLAGS TO WATCH FOR

When evaluating suppliers, be cautious of:

⚠️ **MOQ too high:** If they require 1 million+ pieces minimum, not viable for startup
⚠️ **Mold costs excessive:** >$50K for simple brick molds suggests overpricing
⚠️ **Lead times >12 weeks:** We need reasonable turnaround for reorders
⚠️ **No toy experience:** Toy quality standards are specific (safety, durability)
⚠️ **Poor communication:** If they're slow/difficult during sales process, worse during production
⚠️ **No quality certs:** Must have ISO and preferably toy safety certifications
⚠️ **Vague pricing:** "It depends" without providing ranges suggests they're guessing
⚠️ **Pressure tactics:** "Limited time offer" or "need commitment now" = red flag
