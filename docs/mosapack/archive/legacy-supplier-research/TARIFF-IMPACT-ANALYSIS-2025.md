| **August 2025** | 34% | Post-truce reduced rate |
| **October 2025** | **34-134%** | Volatile - depends on ongoing negotiations |

**Sources:**
- CNN Business: "145% tariffs on China are clobbering the toy industry" (April 2025)
- CNBC: "Toy prices could jump 50% following Trump's tariffs" (April 2025)

#### Financial Impact on Brick Kit
