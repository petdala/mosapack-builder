# Magnet Dropship & Sourcing Strategy

## Executive Summary

**Decision:** Include pre-cut magnets with stickers at $29.99 to maximize conversion, accept lower margin (~40%) to drive volume

**Zero-Inventory Options:**
1. **Dropship pre-cut magnets** (Month 1-3, low volume)
2. **Bulk magnetic sheets** (Month 4+, high volume, 50+ units/month)
3. **In-house cutting** (Month 6+, very high volume, 100+ units/month)

---

## Product Specifications

**Per Kit:**
- 96 mosaic tiles (matches Printful sticker sheet)
- Tile size: ~8mm x 8mm (LEGO 1x1 brick compatible)
- Adhesive backing: Customer applies stickers to magnets

**Magnetic Sheet Size:**
- Standard: 8.5" x 11" (215.9mm x 279.4mm)
- Thickness: 15-30 mil (0.38-0.76mm)
- Type: Flexible rubber magnet with adhesive backing

## Pivot Triggers

**When to Switch from Dropship → Bulk Sheets:**
- Selling 30+ magnet kits/month consistently
- Profit loss from dropship = $7.50 × 30 = **$225/month**
- Bulk investment payback: 1-2 months

**When to Switch from Self-Cutting → Die-Cut Service:**
- Spending >5 hours/month cutting magnets
- Your time worth >$20/hour
- Selling 50+ units/month

**When to Switch from Service → In-House Equipment:**
- Selling 100+ units/month
- Equipment ROI: $500 equipment ÷ $4 savings/unit = 125 units (~2-3 months)

**Scenario C: Stickers + Bulk Magnets (Month 4+)**

✅ Magnets increase conversion by **67%** (3% → 5%)

## Conclusion

**Recommendation:** Launch with dropship magnets, pivot to bulk sheets at 30 units/month.

**Key Insight:** Magnets are a **conversion multiplier**, not a profit center. Accept 15% margin initially to validate the model, then scale to 40% margin with bulk purchasing.

**Critical Success Factor:** Track conversion rate religiously. If magnets don't increase conversion to 4-5%, pivot back to stickers-only.
