# SVG Template Library Insight

## Summary

The SVG market is large and competitive. A generic SVG mega-bundle shop would distract from the MosaPack launch funnel.

## Relevant Niche

Mosaic, layered, and mystery-reveal SVGs are more relevant because they connect directly to the product experience and customer promise.

## Best MosaPack Application

Use SVG/template assets inside the Digital Mystery Reveal Pack:

- printable grid
- SVG/Cricut-compatible cut template
- PDF instruction guide
- color-by-number/mystery reveal files

## Later Opportunity

An Etsy or digital download expansion may make sense only after the core MosaPack funnel proves demand.

## Decision

Do not start a separate SVG business now. Keep SVG/template work attached to the Digital Mystery Reveal Pack and exact-design-save workflow.
