# Magnetic Kit Feasibility Decision

## Concept

A physical magnetic mosaic kit would likely combine:

- printed grid template
- pre-cut or kiss-cut magnetic color sheets
- instructions
- optional display/backing materials

## Feasibility

The concept is feasible, but there does not appear to be a single clean turnkey provider that can produce the full kit, cut format, packaging, and fulfillment workflow exactly as needed today.

## Likely Fulfillment Model

Early fulfillment is likely hybrid:

- POD or printed template vendor for instructions/grid files
- magnet supplier for printed or blank magnetic sheets
- manual kitting or 3PL for first tests
- later supplier consolidation only after samples pass

## Risks

- two packages or split supplier shipments
- separate shipping costs
- assembly friction
- magnet strength
- alignment tolerance
- color consistency
- landed margin
- customer handling issues with small pieces

## Size Tests

- 8mm / LEGO-compatible high-detail test: higher detail, but likely fiddly.
- 3/4 inch easier-assembly test: lower detail, but more usable for families and gift buyers.

## Supplier Categories

- StickerYou
- Printful
- Maghard
- JASDI
- Titan MagMosaic as market/reference pattern
- Discount Magnet / Master Magnetics for prototype materials

## Decision

Physical magnetic kits remain custom proof/quote paths until sample, assembly, shipping, and landed-cost gates pass. No instant physical checkout should launch before those gates are complete.
