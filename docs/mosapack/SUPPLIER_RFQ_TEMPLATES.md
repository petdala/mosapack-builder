# Supplier RFQ Templates

## StickerYou RFQ

Subject: RFQ - Custom kiss-cut square magnetic mosaic sheets

Hello,

We are evaluating suppliers for custom magnetic mosaic kits. Can you support printed kiss-cut or perforated square magnet sheets where the pieces stay attached in the sheet until the customer removes them?

Please provide details on:

- minimum square size
- recommended square size for customer handling
- sheet size options
- material thickness
- magnet strength
- print/color method
- color matching/tolerance
- MOQ
- sample cost
- unit pricing at 1 / 10 / 50 / 100 / 500 / 1000
- dropship support
- white-label/branding options
- packaging options
- lead time
- shipping method
- whether two-package bundling is supported
- file requirements
- alignment tolerance for grid layouts
- reprint/defect policy

Target use case: custom pet photo mosaics with a printed grid template and removable square magnetic pieces.

Thank you.
